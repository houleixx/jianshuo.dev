VoiceDrop 隐私政策

最近更新：2026 年 6 月 20 日
一句话：你的录音是你的。VoiceDrop 不卖、不分析、不拿去做广告，也不接入任何第三方追踪。

我们收集什么

  • 录音：你主动按下录制时产生的音频。录音会上传到属于你自己的私有存储空间（按你的账户隔离），并镜像到你的 iCloud。除了为你存储和回传，我们不会以任何其他目的访问它。
  • 位置（可选）：若你授权定位，App 会把当前位置反向地理编码成一个粗略地名（城市/城区），仅用于给录音文件命名，方便日后辨认。精确坐标不会被存储或上传。你可以拒绝定位，录音照常工作。
  • 麦克风：用于录音，这是 App 的核心功能。
  • 身份标识：为了把文件归到你名下，App 使用一个匿名标识符，或「通过 Apple 登录」返回的稳定匿名标识（Apple sub）。我们不要求、也不存储你的姓名或邮箱，没有密码。

我们不做什么

  • 不向任何第三方出售或共享你的录音与数据。
  • App 内没有广告 SDK、没有第三方分析或追踪。
  • 不为投放广告而追踪你跨 App / 跨网站的行为。

数据存储与删除

录音存放在 Cloudflare R2（你的专属前缀下）并镜像到你的 iCloud。你可以随时在文件中转站删除单条录音。若希望删除全部数据，邮件联系 jianshuo@hotmail.com，我会尽快处理。

儿童

VoiceDrop 不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会主动收集其信息。

联系方式

任何隐私相关问题：jianshuo@hotmail.com（王建硕 / Jianshuo Wang）。

VoiceDrop Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026
In one line: your recordings are yours. VoiceDrop does not sell, analyze, or advertise against them, and includes no third-party tracking.

What we collect

  • Recordings — the audio you create by tapping record. Recordings are uploaded to a private storage area that belongs to you (scoped to your account) and mirrored to your iCloud. We never access them for any purpose other than storing them and returning them to you.
  • Location (optional) — if you grant location access, the app reverse-geocodes your current position into a coarse place name (city / district) used only to name the recording file so you can recognize it later. Precise coordinates are never stored or transmitted. You can deny location and recording still works.
  • Microphone — used to record audio, the app's core function.
  • Identifier — to scope your files to you, the app uses an anonymous identifier, or the stable anonymous identifier (Apple "sub") returned by Sign in with Apple. We do not request or store your name or email, and there is no password.

What we don't do

  • We never sell or share your recordings or data with third parties.
  • The app contains no advertising SDKs and no third-party analytics or tracking.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites for advertising.

Storage & deletion

Recordings are stored in Cloudflare R2 (under your own prefix) and mirrored to your iCloud. You can delete any recording from the file inbox at any time. To delete all of your data, email jianshuo@hotmail.com and I'll take care of it promptly.

Children

VoiceDrop is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their information.

Contact

For any privacy question: jianshuo@hotmail.com (Jianshuo Wang / 王建硕).
