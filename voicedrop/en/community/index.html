Rule 01

What is "feeding a coin"? One sentence

Feeding a coin = a reader tapping the "Feed a coin" button under your article. It's free for the reader and spends none of their writing credits; after the tap, the author gets +1 coin and the reader gets +1 too. Each reader counts once per article per day.

Rule 02

Likes rank, they don't pay

Likes only drive quality and recommendation ranking — they mint no coins. No one has a reason to farm likes, so the leaderboard stays honest and clean.

Rule 03

One daily pool, shared network-wide

The system releases one fixed pool per day. If T coins are minted network-wide that day, each coin is worth pool ÷ T.

Rule 04

Nightly settlement, credits delivered

Every night at settlement: your coins × that day's price = writing credits, delivered. Sleep, and wake up to a topped-up writing balance.

Rule 05

Writing credits = fuel to write more

The in-app quota used to generate articles. Not currency, not withdrawable, not points — what you save up simply means a few more free articles.

Gameplay preview · Rule design

Helping each other wins; gaming it doesn't

In designing Feed-a-Coin mining, we deliberately made the rules abuse-proof yet friendly to mutual support: likes only rank and mint no coins, so no one farms likes; coin-feeding counts once per pair per day, so spamming earns nothing extra; the rare suspicious account gets asked to re-verify with an Apple ID. Inviting friends, reading each other's articles, and feeding each other coins is exactly the play we encourage.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What works today, and what's still in the making — spelled out.

What can I actually do in the app today?+

Recording starts the moment you open it. You talk, and AI writes your words into a polished, publish-ready article; you can read it in the app, revise it by voice, then send it to your WeChat Official Account drafts in one tap or share it to the VD community. All of this exists today, and it's free.

Is Feed-a-Coin mining live yet?+

Not yet — it's in development. Everything on this page about coins, coin-feeding, nightly settlement, early-bird perks, and leaderboards is a gameplay preview / concept demo, not a current feature of the app, and the final rules may change.

Can writing credits be cashed out?+

No. Writing credits are an in-app quota for generating articles (each article uses a little). They're not currency, not withdrawable, not loyalty points — their value is in being spent, on writing a few more pieces. There is no official partnership with or endorsement by Anthropic or Claude.

I can't write articles — will this be hard?+

Then it's exactly for you. VoiceDrop's whole point is "you just talk": recording starts when you open the app, and AI turns your speech into a publish-ready article. You do the talking, AI does the writing — and if you're not happy, say so and it revises.
